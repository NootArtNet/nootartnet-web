NootArtNet is an experimental art project that cannot be called art.

Why is it not called NotArtNet?
Because we think NootArtNet looks a little more special, that's all.

We think that whatever you find valuable is art, even if it is a pile of rubbish.

So, do you see this content as art? Probably yes, but most likely not.

Whatever, it's just for fun.

NootArtNet is a set of character images generated based on the date of each day.

Naming style : ["date number(MMDD)" + "name"], date and name may or may not be related.

There are 367 images in total, only one for each image.

NootArtNet is only sold on OpenSea.
